Capybara

Did you know that capybaras can hold their breath underwater for up to five minutes?

  • Scientific Name: Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris
  • Average Weight: 48.9 kg
  • Average Height: 50 cm
  • Average Length: 130 cm

Known as 'king of the grasslands', the capybara holds the distinction of being the world's largest rodent. These noble, semi-aquatic mammals are native to South America, where they roam the savannas and rain forests--typically in social groups of about 10-20.
